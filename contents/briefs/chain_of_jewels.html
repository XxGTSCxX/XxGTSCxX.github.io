UI/UX Designer

For one of my design electives, I took the User Experience module and from there learnt the basics in UI/UX Design, including different fidelities of wireframes, basic colour theory, spacing and alignment, etc.

For our final project, we got into teams of 2 to 3 members to design and implement a prototype for a hypothetical game, Chain of Jewels. I had the opportunity to try my hand on implementing things like:

  • Mid-Fidelity Wireframes
  • Photobashing Mockups
  • Prototyping Layouts with Unity
